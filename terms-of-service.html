Mind-Blowing Facts Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026

Mind-Blowing Facts is a content service for a small number of connected creator accounts on YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. Account owners opt in by connecting their account, and the service publishes original short-form educational video content to that account on their behalf.

Use of the app

Once a creator connects their account via each platform's official OAuth flow, the app publishes original content to that account using the relevant platform's API (for example, TikTok's Content Posting API). A creator can disconnect their account at any time to stop this.

All content published by this app is original — narration, scripts, and titles are written specifically for this purpose. Any third-party stock footage used is licensed for the intended use.
