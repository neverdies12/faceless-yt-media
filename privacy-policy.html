Mind-Blowing Facts Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Mind-Blowing Facts is a content service that generates short-form educational "fact" videos and publishes them to a small number of connected creator accounts on YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram, on behalf of the people who own those accounts. Creators choose to connect their account to receive this content; the app does not operate on any account without that account's owner authorizing it first.

What this app does

For each connected account, the app generates original video content and publishes it using the relevant platform's official API (for example, TikTok's Content Posting API), under the authorization each account owner grants during sign-in.

Data collected

For each connected account, the app stores the account identifier (for example, TikTok's open_id) and the OAuth access/refresh tokens issued by that platform, scoped to the minimum permissions needed to post content (for example, TikTok's user.info.basic and video.publish scopes). No other personal data is collected, and no data from outside the connected platforms is accessed.

Data sharing

No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party. Each account's authentication tokens are used only to publish content to that same account, and are never used to access or act on any other account's data.

Data retention

Authentication tokens are retained only for as long as an account stays connected to the service, and can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the app from that platform's account settings — doing so immediately stops any further posting to that account.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer via the contact information on file with each platform's developer portal.
