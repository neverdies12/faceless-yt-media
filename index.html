Mind-Blowing Facts

Mind-Blowing Facts is a small content service: we generate short, original educational fact videos — science, history, psychology — and publish them daily to a handful of connected creator accounts across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.

How it works

Each video pairs an original narration script with licensed stock footage and captions. A creator connects one or more of their social accounts through that platform's own official sign-in flow, and from then on new videos are published to their account automatically. No account is ever touched without its owner authorizing the connection first, and any creator can disconnect at any time.

Who it's for: right now, a small number of creators who wanted a hands-off way to keep a "daily facts" channel active without producing every video themselves. It's not a public self-serve signup yet — reach out via the contact details on file with each platform's developer portal to inquire about connecting an account.

Sample facts
